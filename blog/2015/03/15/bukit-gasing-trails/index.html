Bukit Gasing Trails

Mar 15th, 2015

Bukit Gasing Trails was a website I created showing the various hiking trails that exist in the Bukit Gasing Forest Park.

At the time the site was built only partial information about trail routes was available online. However, in recent years the park’s trails and points of interest have been added to Open Street Map. Since OSM provides more information and will continue to do so (given its crowd-sourced nature) I have decided to bring Bukit Gasing Trails down.

The site’s source code is still available on Github. Building it exposed me to the world of static websites generators like Jekyll, and to the Google Maps API. If you’d like to see what the site looked like, here it is:
