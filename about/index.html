About

I was born and brought up in Shetland, Scotland. After finishing school I went to study Mathematics at the University of Edinburgh. After graduating I felt a change of direction was necessary and so moved into web development and programming. I met my now wife during university and we decided to settle down in Malaysia where she’s from. We currently live in the country’s capital, Kuala Lumpur, and I currently work as a Software Developer at Mindvalley. For more information about what I do and have done, check out the projects page.

I like to use simple, lightweight yet powerful development tools. The OS I use is Debian Linux. I do almost everything on a Bash shell, use Vim (the last editor you’ll ever use) for writing code and Git/Github for source control.
